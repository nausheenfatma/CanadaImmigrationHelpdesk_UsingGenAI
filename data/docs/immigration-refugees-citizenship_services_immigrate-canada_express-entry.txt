Express Entry
 
Express Entry is an online system that we use to manage immigration applications from skilled workers.
 
There are 3 immigration programs managed through Express Entry:
 - Canadian Experience Class
 - Federal Skilled Worker Program
 - Federal Skilled Trades Program
 
How the Express Entry process works:
 - Create a profile and enter the pool.
 - We'll invite the candidates with the most points in rounds.
 - If you're invited to apply, fill out the application.
 - We'll review your application and make a decision.



Processing times
 
Varies by program (Refer page: /en/immigration-refugees-citizenship/services/application/check-processing-times.html)



Fees
 
Your application: $CAN 1,525
 
Fees for your family members:
 - Spouse: $CAN 1,525
 - Dependent child: $CAN 260
